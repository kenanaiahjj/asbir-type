# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

The primary users are a design team shaping a new typeface into a reliable release. They need to inspect letterforms, tune a compact set of family-level decisions, compare writing samples, and hand off dependable production files to product and engineering teams.

## Product Purpose

Asbir Sans Studio is the working environment for the Asbir Sans type family: an original geometric neo-grotesk designed for user interfaces and display work. Success is a disciplined source-of-truth workflow that makes the family’s visual decisions legible and its export state unambiguous.

## Positioning

One calm, exact place to refine an original UI typeface and release the files a product team can trust.

## Brand Personality

Measured, architectural, and quietly confident. The family should sit between the neutral utility of Inter and the crisp geometry of Geist without imitating either.

## Anti-references

Avoid a visual clone of Inter or Geist, decorative type-editor theatrics, and obscure export states that make release readiness unclear.

## Design Principles

- Let the letterforms be the principal visual material.
- Keep important production information visible without turning the workspace into a dashboard.
- Make every control explain a typographic decision in its immediate context.
- Separate editable exploration from release-ready output.
- Prefer calm precision over ornamental complexity.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, provide reduced-motion support, and avoid color-only status cues. Controls must remain usable with keyboard navigation and color-vision differences.
